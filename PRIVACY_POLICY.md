# Privacy Policy for MCVenture

**Last Updated: November 24, 2025**

## Introduction

MCVenture ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MCVenture (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: We collect your real-time GPS location when you use the trip tracking feature
- **Route History**: We store your traveled routes on your device
- **Waypoints**: We save locations you mark during your trips
- **Purpose**: To provide real-time navigation, track your rides, and calculate trip statistics

### 2. Personal Information
- **Profile Data**: Name, email address (optional)
- **Emergency Contacts**: Phone numbers and names of emergency contacts you provide
- **Preferences**: App settings and customization choices
- **Purpose**: To provide personalized experience and emergency features

### 3. Trip Data
- **Ride Statistics**: Distance, speed, duration, elevation
- **Photos**: Images you capture during trips (with location data)
- **Waypoints and Notes**: Information you add to your routes
- **Purpose**: To provide analytics and trip history

### 4. Device Information
- **Device Type**: iPhone/iPad model
- **Operating System**: iOS version
- **App Version**: MCVenture version number
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data if the app crashes
- **Purpose**: To improve app performance and fix bugs

### 5. Usage Data
- **Feature Usage**: Which features you use and how often
- **App Performance**: Load times, errors, crashes
- **Purpose**: To improve the app and understand user needs

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Track your motorcycle trips with GPS
   - Calculate route statistics (distance, speed, elevation)
   - Provide navigation and route guidance
   - Save your trip history

2. **Safety Features**
   - Detect potential crashes
   - Send emergency alerts to your contacts
   - Share your location with emergency contacts when needed

3. **Social Features**
   - Share routes with other users via CloudKit
   - Display community-shared routes
   - Enable social feed interactions

4. **Analytics and Improvements**
   - Generate trip statistics and insights
   - Improve app performance
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Develop new features

5. **Communication**
   - Send important app updates
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Notify about new features

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Most data stays on your device**: Trip history, routes, photos, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone/iPad
- **No cloud backup by default**: Your data is not automatically uploaded to our servers

### CloudKit Storage (Optional)
- **User-Shared Routes**: If you choose to share routes, they are stored in Apple's CloudKit
- **Apple Privacy Standards**: CloudKit data is encrypted and follows Apple's privacy policies
- **User Control**: You can delete shared routes at any time

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted on your device
- **No Third-Party Access**: We do not sell or share your data with third parties
- **Secure Connections**: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
