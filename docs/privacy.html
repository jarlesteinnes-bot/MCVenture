Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

MCVenture ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

MCVenture collects and uses your device's location data to provide core navigation and tracking features:

  • When in Use: Used for turn-by-turn navigation and route display
  • Background Location: Used to continue tracking your trip even when the app is in the background
  • GPS Coordinates: Collected to record your routes and calculate distance, speed, and elevation

Motion & Fitness Data

  • Accelerometer data for crash detection
  • Trip statistics (distance, speed, elevation)

Photos & Media

  • Photos you choose to attach to trips or routes
  • Photos saved to your device from the app

User-Generated Content

  • Routes you create or save
  • Trip recordings and waypoints
  • Ratings and reviews you submit
  • Notes and annotations

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide turn-by-turn navigation and route guidance
  • Record and display your motorcycle trips
  • Calculate trip statistics (distance, speed, elevation)
  • Detect potential crashes and enable emergency features
  • Show weather alerts for your current location
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Trip recordings and statistics
  • Saved routes and favorites
  • App settings and preferences

iCloud Storage

With your permission, we use Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices:

  • Your routes and favorites
  • Trip recordings
  • App preferences

This data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Security Measures

  • All location data is stored securely on your device
  • Cloud sync uses Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure
  • No personally identifiable information is collected without your consent

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Route Sharing (Optional)

When you choose to share a route with other users:

  • Only the route coordinates and basic information are shared
  • Your personal information is not included
  • You control what you share and with whom

Emergency Services

If you activate the Emergency SOS feature:

  • Your current location may be shared with emergency services
  • This only occurs when you explicitly activate the SOS feature

5. Third-Party Services

MCVenture uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple Maps: For map display and route visualization
  • CloudKit: For data synchronization across your devices
  • Weather Service: For weather alerts and forecasts

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app in Settings
  • Delete: Delete individual trips, routes, or all app data
  • Export: Export your trip data in GPX format
  • Disable: Turn off location services, crash detection, or CloudKit sync at any time

Location Permissions

You can change location permissions at any time in your device Settings:

  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → MCVenture

7. Children's Privacy

MCVenture is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification for significant changes

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@mcventure.app
  • GitHub: github.com/yourusername/mcventure

10. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent for location tracking and data storage
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide the app's core functionality
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve app performance and user experience

11. Data Retention

  • Trip Data: Stored indefinitely on your device until you delete it
  • CloudKit Data: Stored in your iCloud account until you delete it
  • Crash Reports: Automatically deleted after 30 days

To delete all data: Settings → Delete All Data or uninstall the app.
